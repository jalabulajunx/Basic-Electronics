What is Electric Power?

Power is the rate at which energy is transferred or used. Just like how water flows through a pipe, electricity flows through wires - and power tells us how much work that electricity can do every second!

Power vs. Energy

Power is the rate of using energy. If energy is like water in a bucket, power is how fast you pour the water.

The Watt

The watt (W) is named after James Watt, the inventor who improved the steam engine in the 1700s!

Fun Fact

Did you know? A typical lightning bolt has about 1 billion watts of power - enough to light up 100 million LED bulbs!
