What are Inductors?

Inductors are components that store energy in a magnetic field! They're like springs for electricity - they resist changes in current and can create magnetic fields. Think of them as electrical inertia!

Magnetic Fields

When current flows through an inductor, it creates a magnetic field. The stronger the current, the stronger the field!
